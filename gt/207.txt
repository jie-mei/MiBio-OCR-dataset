Two nests of the common Willow-Warbler were found and others were seen
building in the Shetlands in May, 1901.

The Wood-Warbler was heard on the Isle of Man, in May 1901 and 1905.

Family-TURDIDÆ.

Subfamily-SYLVIINÆ.

THE RUFOUS WARBLER.

Aedon galactodes, TEMM.

THIS rare species has occurred in Sussex and Devon in the autumn.

Family-TURDIDÆ.

Subfamily-SYLVIINÆ.

THE GREY-BACKED WARBLER.

Aedon familiaris.

A male was shot at Hythe, Kent, on July 15th, 1907.

Family-TURDIDÆ.

Subfamily-SYLVIINÆ.

RADDE'S BUSH-WARBLER.

Lusciniola schwarzi, RADDE.

A specimen was obtained in October, 1898, on the Lincolnshire side of the
Humber, by Mr. Haigh.
